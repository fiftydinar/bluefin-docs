Sometimes it can be [frustrating](https://github.com/ublue-os/bluefin/discussions/4118) keeping up with the good stuff. Sometimes it's hard to keep up, so we've been working on better automated reporting tools. This involves a ton of things no one ever wants to do, like ensuring issues have proper labels, etc. But we've tightened all that up and [dosu](https://dosu.dev/) has been labelling our PRs for years now. 

And since every person's contribution is in the log it lets us now programatically just generate our changelogs. Check our [https://docs.projectbluefin.io/reports](https://docs.projectbluefin.io/reports), which are published on the first of each month.
